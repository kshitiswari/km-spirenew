The Company

S P I R E Consultants, is a Bhubaneswar based proprietorship firm offering consultancy services in field of architecture, planning, engineering, interior design, landscaping, electrical and plumbing.

Spire has its mission objectives in applying modern, analytical, quantitative techniques and systems approach to architecture & planning in the seamless execution of its projects.

S P I R E has so far executed numerous assignments. Its services have always been characterized by Objectivity, Efficiency, Economy, Quality and Timely completion. Cost effective technology solutions in Architectural and planning projects are one of the major areas where the achievements of SPIRE have been particularly recognized.

Sources

The office is well equipped with the necessary infrastructure like personal computing systems, drawing and printing equipment and is supported by an efficient team of technical and managerial staff. It takes assistance from other specialized consulting firms whenever necessary, in areas such as Structural design, Soil testing and Exploration, Electrical, Air-Conditioning, Sanitary and Plumbing works.

S P I R E is also associated with other outstation firms at New Delhi (VASTUKRITI) and Bangalore (ARCHPLAN) for execution of projects warranting specialized services.
